## Notes

- Some evaluator plan labels do not exactly match the vendor's current public marketing copy. For example, OpenAI now commonly presents the self-serve team workspace as `ChatGPT Business`, while the evaluator prompt still says `ChatGPT Team`.
- Enterprise plans are not normalized to invented prices in the engine. For those, user-entered monthly spend remains the source of truth.
- API-direct tools are modeled from actual monthly spend entered by the user, because most startup teams know the invoice before they know their exact token mix.
